## Example 1: Wealth Management Director at a GCC Bank

**Target:** Head of Private Banking, [Bank Name], UAE

**Step 1 — LinkedIn connection:**
```
[Name], I noticed [Bank]'s recent expansion into ultra-HNW client services in Abu Dhabi — great move given where the market is heading. Would love to connect.
```

**Step 2 — LinkedIn follow-up (Day 4):**
```
[Name],

[Bank]'s AUM growth of X% last year caught my eye. Given the shift toward more sophisticated client demands in the region, I imagine wealth advisory teams are under real pressure to upskill fast.

That's exactly what we help banks address — practical, outcomes-focused programmes for relationship managers and advisors.

Worth a 20-minute call to share what's worked at similar institutions?

[Signature]
```

**Step 3 — Email (Day 8):**
```
Subject: [Bank] + talent development — quick question

[Name],

Following up on my LinkedIn message. I wanted to share one more thought.

[Bank]'s recent focus on [specific initiative] suggests the team is evolving quickly. That usually creates a gap between strategy and execution — something we've helped [peer bank] close in [timeframe].

Happy to share specifics if useful. 15 minutes?

[Signature]
```
