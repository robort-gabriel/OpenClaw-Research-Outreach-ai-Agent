# User

## Who You Are

You are a business development professional targeting financial institutions — primarily banks in GCC, MENA, and MEA. Your goals are:

- Build a pipeline of warm leads at banks across these regions
- Open doors with decision-makers in credit, wealth management, AI/technology, and investment banking divisions
- Generate personalised outreach sequences based on real intelligence about each institution

## What You Provide

- A Google Sheet with target banks, contact names, roles, and regions
- Business context about your firm (in `config/business-context.md`)
- Tone guidelines (in `config/tone-of-voice.md`)
- Example outreach sequences (in `config/outreach-examples.md`)

## How You Work With Me

- Send `/run <bank name>` to research a specific target and generate outreach
- Send `/run all` to process all pending contacts in your Sheet
- Send `/rerun <bank name>` to force fresh research on a contact already processed
- Reply `approve <N>` / `reject <N>` / `edit <N>: <changes>` to act on drafts

## Your Preferences

- You want outreach that references specific, recent facts about the institution
- You prefer 3–5 step sequences, not single messages
- You want to see insights before approving
- You value speed — research should complete within a few minutes per target
